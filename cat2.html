Bluetooth Mate
Bluetooth USB Adapter

Wireless communication is achieved using Bluetooth Mate Silver(WRL-12576 RoHS) and Bluetooth USB Adapter – Parani-ud100. This is a great wireless replacement for serial cables. Data transmission from PC to INX-Z30(with Bluetooth Mate) is achieved and vice versa, which is as shown below. Interface, Bluetooth Mate with INX-Z30 is nothing but UART communication.

Interfacing of Bluetooth with the MCU is as shown in figure. VCC and GND are provided to the Bluetooth Mate from INX-Z30. TX-RX of INX-Z30 is connected with the RX-TX pins of the Bluetooth Mate.
